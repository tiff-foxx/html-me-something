Tiffany Foxx - Places I've visited

  • London, England
  • Istanbul, Turkey
  • New Delhi, India
  • Athens, Greece
  • Tokyo, Japan

London, England

London is a cosmopolitan city with people from every corner of the world (you can thank the British Empire for that). The story of Henry VII and Anne Boelyn never gets old. I love the history there.

Istanbul, Turkey

Istanbul is a beautiful modern city that has not lost touch with the past. The city features a varied past. The religions of it's conquerers can be seen throught the city's architecture. The € was not used here. The Turkish Lira was instead.

New Delhi, India

New Delhi was my first stop in India, but I also made it to Agra and Jaipur. This is easily one of the most intriguing and beautiful countries I've visited. The environment is harsh, but it's a small price to pay in exchange for the grandeur that is India.

Athens, Greece

Athens was bursting with history, great food, and great people. While it was very hot I enjoyed visting the ancient sites and wondering whose footsteps had also crossed the paths that I was walking in this old, old city.

Tokyo, Japan

Tokyo is a place I've wanted to visit since middle school. I will never get tired of this place and hope to have a home here one day. Tokyo is something indescribable. You'll have to go there yourself.
